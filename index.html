Our Story

At CICA, our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to protect your skin while you embrace the great outdoors. Founded by a group of passionate sun lovers and skincare enthusiasts, we recognized the need for effective sun protection that doesn’t compromise on quality or environmental responsibility.

Our story is rooted in innovation and care. After countless hours of research and development, we created a line of sun creams that not only shield your skin from harmful UV rays but also nourish and hydrate. We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy the sun safely, which is why our products are crafted with natural ingredients, free from harmful chemicals, and suitable for all skin types.

As we grew, so did our commitment to sustainability. We source our ingredients ethically and use eco-friendly packaging to minimize our impact on the planet. Our goal is to inspire a community that values both skin health and environmental stewardship.

Join us on this journey to celebrate the sun while protecting what matters most—your skin and our planet. Together, we can make every sunny day a safe and joyful experience!
